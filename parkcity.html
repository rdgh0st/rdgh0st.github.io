Park City Mountain

Park City Mountain is located just outside of Salt Lake City, Utah. Along with purgatory, it is one of the most popular destinations in the US for snow sports.

  • Park City Mountain is very split between two peaks, where each of them has a variety of routes and difficulties available. There is a middle section that connects these regions.
  • There are two main basins corresponding with each of peaks, and there are several chairlifts throughout the mountain that allow riders to reach the highest points.
  • Generally, the higher you go on this mountain the more difficult the runs get. The runs down from the precipice of both peaks are only double black diamonds.

Park City is also an excellent mountain for experienced riders who are looking to explore throughout the mountain for a couple days, and who are comfortable doing difficult routes.
