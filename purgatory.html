Purgatory Ski Resort

Purgatory Ski Resort is located in the southwest of Colorado. There is extensive lodging around the mountain with varying price points. Purgatory is one of the most popular destinations in the US for snow sports.

  • The main area of purgatory is split into sections with easier tracks and harder tracks. On the right, there are green and blue routes while on the left there are black and blue routes.
  • There are two restaurants atop the mountain. They offer lookouts over the rest of the mountain and Durango. At the base of the mountain is more lodges and a hub area with various shops and more activities.
  • There are also more terrain parks around the mountain, allowing those who feel confident in their abilities to try different tricks. There are rails, jumps, boxes, and more on differing slope levels in order create a diverse environment for tricks

Purgatory is a gorgeous mountain perfect for those looking for a premier snow sports experience. Anything an experienced rider could want will be found here, and there are options for beginners as well.
